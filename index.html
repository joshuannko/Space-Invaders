How to Play


  1) Use the arrow keys (⇦ ⇨) to move the cannon; and spacebar to fire.

  2) Eliminate all invaders to complete a level. If any invader passes the cannon, one life is lost.

  3) The game ends when all lives are lost and the aliens invade the planet.
